Travel & Transportation

Airports

We recommend Harrisburg International for the shortest drive, but all three airports are convenient depending on your travel plans.

In order of proximity:

  • Harrisburg International Airport (MDT) – approximately 26 minutes away
  • Lancaster Airport (LNS) – approximately 1 hour away
  • Philadelphia International Airport (PHL) – approximately 2 hours away
