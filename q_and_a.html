Questions & Answers

But what about...

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by March 1.

Can I bring a date?

Please check if your invitation includes a guest! If so, this will be written on the envelope.

Where is the venue?

The Manor at Mountain View is located at 2201 Fishing Creek Valley Road, Harrisburg, PA 17112.

Where should I park?

When arriving at the venue, please park across the street from the venue in the larger parking lot (look for signs). There will be golf carts to shuttle you across the street.

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony and cocktail hour will be outdoors, weather permitting, with the reception indoors. We recommend planning attire and footwear accordingly.

What will the weather be like?

Early April in Harrisburg can be unpredictable, with cool mornings and evenings and mild afternoons. We recommend dressing in layers and bringing a light jacket or wrap, especially for the outdoor ceremony and cocktail hour. Umbrellas will be provided if needed!

Can I take photos or videos during the wedding?

We kindly ask that guests remain unplugged during the ceremony and refrain from taking photos or videos. Feel free to take as many as you want during cocktail hour and the reception, and share them using the QR code on the back of your place card.
